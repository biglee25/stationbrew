We are a cosy cafe in the heart of Greenfield, opposite the train station. Serving award-winning Darkwood's coffee, Tea Punks, locally blended teas, breakfasts, lunch and cake to eat in or takeaway. High quality ingredients from local suppliers. Vegan options.

About Our Products

We love to shop local and support our local businesses - and we thank you for doing the same. Here are some of the lovely places we source our high quality ingredients.

  • Artisan breads from Rogers Bakery, Marsden. Roger’s ‘Seeded Malthouse’ & ‘Overnight White’ won 2018 Great Taste Awards!

  • Breakfast muffins from J.W Buckleys in Uppermill.

  • Free range eggs, fresh milk and cream from McLintock’s Dairy, Denshaw.

  • Fresh fruit and veg from Jim Stanford’s Greengrocers, Greenfield.

  • Award-winning coffee from Darkwood's Roastery in Marsden.

Our Commitment to the Environment


We are fully commited to keeping our business as eco-friendly as possible.

We use completely compostable take out cups and packaging to play our part in minimising the huge amount of plastic packaging that ends up in landfill and in our oceans every year.


Our packaging is made from plants and composts best when put in your food waste bins. We pay a bit more for this but believe that it’s worth every penny.


We offer 15p off take-out drinks if you bring your own reusable cup. We sell Ecoffecup reusable cups, made from sustainable bamboo.


Instead of buying a plastic bottle of water, why not ask us for a glass of tap water or we would be happy to fill up your bottle for you free of charge.
